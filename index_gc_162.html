 FAQ
How frequently is this updated ?
Depends. It could update every minute, 5 minutes, or every 15 minutes max.
Is there D2MT for other browsers ?
Yes, Chrome - Firefox - Opera - Web
Does this run as a background process ?
No. Unlike many extensions, this one does not. As soon as you close the extension, you are freeing resource. (Except for firefox)
What channels are you taking VODs from ?
VODs section will show you the 15 most recent uploads from the most popular DotA2 channels (currently 12). Contact me if you want to add yours.
Why are some news bolded ?
It's to show news that are hot. (Over 50 comments)
Joindota has points not elo, what gives ?
I devided the points by 22 to give the same elo feeling that GosuGamers gives.

Outpost Travel

 Planning to attend The International 3 in person ? Living in North America ? Outpost is here to nerf those hefty prices into something more affordable for the average Dota player. Just go to Outpost and input your location to where you want to travel (in this case it would be Seattle). You will find rideshares going to any city and place rentals posted by house owners. No more having a headache to rent a car or booking expensive hotels.

Outpost is a personal side project to help Dota players afford to attend TI3. It could be used for any other purpose. Let me know what you think at info@outpost.travel
